About Us

SOFTGENE TECHNOLOGIES is dedicated to empowering aspiring
developers through personalized mentoring and guidance.
Founded in 2016, we have successfully mentored hundreds of students, helping them build
essential skills and launch successful careers in web development.
Our team consists of experienced professionals who are passionate about
nurturing talent and fostering a supportive learning environment.
At SOFTGENE TECHNOLOGIES, we are committed to excellence,
continuous improvement, and the success of each of our mentees on their
journey to becoming proficient web developers.
